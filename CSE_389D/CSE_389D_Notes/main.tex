\subsection{An Electric Bill Optimization Problem}
Suppose we wish to find a function $x(t)$ to minimize $\int_0^{24} c(t) dt$
where
\begin{equation*}
    c(t) = \begin{cases}
        (0.16) x(t) & 0 \leq t \leq 8 \text{ or $21 \leq t \leq 24$}  \\
        g((x(t)) & 8 \leq t \leq 21 \\
    \end{cases}
\end{equation*}

where 
\begin{equation*}
    g(x(t)) = \begin{cases}
        0.25x(t) - 0.3 & \text{ if $x(t) > 10$ and $10 \leq t \leq 16$} \\
        -0.3 & \text{ if $x(t)  < 10$ and $10 \leq t \leq 16$} \\
        0.25x(t) & \text{otherwise}
    \end{cases}
\end{equation*}

with the constraint that $\int_0^{24}x(t)dt = 100$ and additionally that $x(t) < 20$ for any given $t$. This is a resonable assumption for a given household.
